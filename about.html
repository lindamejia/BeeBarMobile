Our Story

Welcome to Bee Bar Mobile, where our family’s buzz-worthy passion and creativity come together to make your events truly the bee's knees!

The idea for our mobile bar service really started to take flight when I, Bobby, was planning a sweet proposal for my queen bee, Alex. My mom, Dana, who’s always been the hive’s wisest worker, suggested: “Everyone will expect Alex to mix drinks since she's a bar pro. How about we hire a service?” But instead of just adding another bee to the hive, we decided to create our own—complete with all the honeyed touches!

With our team of bee-lievers, we transformed a vintage 1994 Horse trailer into a buzzing mobile bar. Here’s the hive of activity behind Bee Bar Mobile:

Alex: My amazing wife, our mixologist extraordinaire, and the queen bee of cocktail creation.

Dana: My mom, our creative director, who brings a splash of art and design with every project.

Mitch: My stepdad, the drink tester and contractor, ensuring everything is just bee-autifully perfect.

Together, we’ve created Bee Bar Mobile—a family-run venture where every event is abuzz with love, laughter, and fantastic drinks. Here’s to making every gathering the bee's knees!

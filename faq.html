FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

General Information

What is a mobile bar service?

Our mobile bar service brings the bar experience to your event location. We provide a fully equipped bar with professional bartenders, ready to serve your guests with a variety of drinks.

What types of events do you cater to?

We cater to a wide range of events including weddings, corporate events, private parties, and more. No matter the occasion, we can customize our service to fit your needs.

What areas do you serve?

We serve a variety of locations. Please contact us for specific details on the areas we cover.

How far in advance should I book your services?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance to ensure availability, especially for peak seasons and popular dates.

Services and Offerings

What services are included in your mobile bar package?

Our package includes a complete bar setup, a variety of drink options, professional bartending services, and both setup and teardown of the bar.

Can I customize the drink menu for my event?

Yes, we offer customizable drink menus to suit your preferences and the theme of your event.

Do you offer non-alcoholic options?

Absolutely! We provide a range of non-alcoholic beverages, including mocktails and soft drinks.

Can you provide your own alcohol, or do we need to supply it?

We can provide alcohol for your event, or you can choose to supply it yourself. We can discuss options to best meet your needs.

Costs and Payments

What is the cost of your mobile bar service?

Costs vary depending on the event size, location, and package details. Please contact us for a personalized quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit cards and bank transfers.

Are there any additional fees we should be aware of?

Additional fees may apply for travel, overtime, or special requests. We will provide a detailed breakdown in your quote.

Setup and Logistics

How much space do you need for the mobile bar setup?

We typically require a space of at least 10x10 feet for the bar setup, but this can vary based on the event size and setup requirements.

How long does the setup and teardown take?

Setup usually takes about 1-2 hours before the event starts, and teardown takes about 1 hour after the event ends.

Do you require access to electricity and water?

Yes, we need access to electricity and, in some cases, water for certain types of setups. We will discuss these requirements with you ahead of time.

What is your policy on last-minute changes or cancellations?

Our policy varies based on the timing and nature of the changes or cancellations. Please review our terms and conditions or contact us for specific details.

Staffing and Service

How many staff members will be present at my event?

The number of staff members depends on the size of your event. We will provide a staffing plan based on your event details.

Are your bartenders trained and certified?

Yes, all of our bartenders are trained and certified to ensure the highest level of service and compliance with legal requirements.

What measures do you take to ensure responsible alcohol service?

We adhere to strict guidelines to ensure responsible alcohol service and comply with all relevant laws and regulations.

Health and Safety

What health and safety measures do you follow?

We follow rigorous health and safety protocols, including hygiene practices and sanitary measures to ensure the safety of your guests.

What happens if there is an emergency or unforeseen issue?

We have contingency plans in place to handle emergencies or unforeseen issues to ensure the smooth operation of your event.

Contact and Communication

How can I contact you for more information or to book a service?

You can reach us via email at beebarmobile@gmail.com or through our contact form on our website.

What is your response time for inquiries?

We typically respond to inquiries within 24-48 hours. If you have an urgent request, please let us know.
